Can I sumulate a mechanical ventilator on MATLAB?

1 About the program

The program simulates a volume control ventilation system. Here, our variable to control is the airflow or volume and our dependent variable is the pressure.  The program request a specific set of input data as a real ventilator does (i.e. tidal volume, rpm, PEEP, I:E ratio, FiO2, Trigger, etc.). The main goal with this simulation is to understand how the three variables (flow, volume and pressure) are displayed on the user interface screen, as a valuable source of information for the experimented clinical staff.

2 Why I am doing this?

I have the pleasure to make part of a non-profit project called LungLife, whose main objective is to design and build a low-cost mechanical ventilation prototype which will be donated to public institutions in both Colombia and Mexico. LungLife team includes a wide range of multidisciplinary professionals, since physicians, anesthesiologists, respiratory therapists and engineers. As a Biomedical Engineer, one of my main function is to liaise as a bridge between the clinical and the technical staff.
